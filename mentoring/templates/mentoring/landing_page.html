You enjoyed your orientation week and would like to give the new students at the Faculty of Mechanical Engineering an equally great start to their studies?

Then sign up as a mentor!

The orientation week always takes place in the first week of October and your task is to accompany the new students during this week. (In the summer semester there is always only an orientation day, which is in the week before lectures start.) We are responsible for the organization of the program. As a preparation you will also receive a crash course with the most important information as well as the procedure of the orientation week. At the end we will certify your activities as a mentor, so that you can write your honorary commitment on your resume.

Do you have any questions? Then write us an email.

We are looking forward to your registration and a nice orientation week.
